Battle Arena

How to Win:

  • Choose cards with higher power for advantage
  • Consider type matchups (Fire > Grass > Water > Fire)
  • Manage your HP - cards with 0 HP are defeated
  • Be the last player with cards remaining
  • Winner gets 100 PokeCoins!

Rules:

  • Each round, both players select a card
  • Higher power deals damage equal to its own power
  • Lower power takes damage equal to opponent's power
  • Equal power: both take opponent's power damage
  • Continue until one player has no cards left
